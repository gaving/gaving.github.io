Five "laws" of human-computer interaction:

* [Fitt's Law (of movement to a target area)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fitts%27s_law)
* [Steering Law (of movement through a tunnel)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Steering_law)
* [Hick's Law (of choices and decision time)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hick%27s_law)
* [Miller's Law (of working memory)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Miller%27s_law)
* [Power Law (of practice and reaction time)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Power_law_of_practice)
